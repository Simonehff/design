ENGIE Brasil

Website redesign - Company repositioning

Team work
2020
Styleguide
Components
UI Design
Figma

Website

ENGIE Brazil is the biggest private energy company from Brazil. The website redesign, besides France Headquarters new guidelines, was also due a new market positioning. Developed by the Mint Interactive Lab.

Amongst the team, two designers, myself and Fabio Dudas, were responsible for the website architecture, styleguide structuration, hi-fi layout, prototipation, jira documentation and interface QA testing.

The company market positioning change was followed by architecture changes such as a new section named solutions. The solutions area gather all ENGIE's products and services, distributed by expertises linked with business segments - such as industry, agribusiness, health, cities and others.

In this project, one of my main tasks was adapt and create new styleguide components: the Brazil website is substantially bigger than France's one, so an big amount of new components were needed in the design system.

Along the design system organization, I've worked around the solutions UI design, components interaction and hi-fi prototype. The website owns around one hundred pages, all of them layouted and prototyped.

The new ENGIE Brazil website also counts with darkmode, page reader and a brazilian sign language interpreter plugin.

Plastubos

Website redesign

Team work
UI/UX Design
hi-fi prototype
2019
Figma

Website

Plastubos is on low price pipes and connections market for over 20 years, with product distribution available in all country. Developed by Mint Interactive Lab, the design team was composed by myself and Teilor Capanema.

The website design project evolved understanding of the user needs, information architecture, wireframes, design system, micro interactions, layout and hi-fi prototypation.

Due brand redesign, Plastubos wanted an coherent digital repositioning, where an website update aimed to close the gap between the company and the costumer, offering along content about it's products and ease the purchasing process.

The website main content and goal is to show and detail the technical information about the Plastubos products, where the main public are mans, with more than 25 years, from C, D and E social class.

To fullfil the use context, the website was developed focused in the most used resolutions in the country: 1366x768 (21.78%) for desktop devices and 360x640 (17.63%) for mobile devices (Statcounter GlobalStats - 2019).

DECOS

Cosmetic component description app interface

Graduation project
Hi-fi prototype
2019
App
Figma

Prototype

The variety of cosmetic products available on the market is wide and growing. Also grows our concern with the quality of what we consume and the possible effects into our bodies.

How to facilitate the cosmetics composition identification so that people can make a decision regarding its use?

Amid the rush and the constant presence of cell phones,

an application makes information accessible quickly and accurately,

ready to deliver content when the user needs it.

As a graduation project, I've made an app design project evolving steps and processes like user research (to delimitate user goals, contents and features), navigation design, information architecture, cardsorting, paper and digital wireframes, user flow tests, styleguide, layout, micro interactions and high fidelity wireframes.
